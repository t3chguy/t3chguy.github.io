Worthless

Apr 17, 2016

I guess I just need to get it out. I’m steadily feeling worse and more isolated. I tried to take my own life, and sadly failed, I spend most days crying and every wishing that I wasn’t me, because frankly I hate everything about me.
I would say that it started when my ex-girlfriend left me, but thats far from the case, I’ve been battling a lesser form of depression for a while, having been on anti-depressants a couple of years back without much improving, I just learnt to keep a strong face and try to hide it.
Its impossible to hide now. With exams approaching, everything is compounding. Everyone has left me. Only time I get people messaging me is when they want or need something. When I desperately need someone to talk to I have no one, sometimes used to get my “best friend” message me but now she has alternate priorities.
